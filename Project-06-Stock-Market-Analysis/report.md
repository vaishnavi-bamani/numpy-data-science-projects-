# 📈 Stock Market Analysis Report

## Project Overview

This project analyzes a 30-day stock market dataset using NumPy. Various financial metrics such as price changes, trading volume, percentage returns, and volatility are calculated.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze stock market prices.
- Study daily market movement.
- Calculate financial statistics.
- Evaluate trading volume.
- Identify bullish and bearish trends.

---

## Dataset Information

- Total Trading Days: 30
- Features:
  - Day
  - Opening Price
  - Highest Price
  - Lowest Price
  - Closing Price
  - Trading Volume

---

## Analysis Performed

- Dataset Exploration
- Closing Price Analysis
- Daily Price Change
- Percentage Return
- Trading Volume Analysis
- Bullish vs Bearish Days
- Price Volatility
- Top Closing Prices
- Percentile Analysis
- Stock Market Dashboard

---

## NumPy Functions Used

- np.genfromtxt()
- np.mean()
- np.max()
- np.min()
- np.std()
- np.sum()
- np.median()
- np.percentile()
- np.sort()
- np.argmax()
- np.argmin()
